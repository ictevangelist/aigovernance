The guidance map

Every framework the policy sits within, what each one requires, and the pages on this site that speak to it.

Home › The guidance map

The Use of AI Policy doesn’t stand on its own. It sits within a body of law, statutory guidance and sector standards. This page maps the whole of it in one place.

Law, statutory guidance and standards referenced by the policy
FrameworkWhat it requiresWhere it lands on this site
Keeping Children Safe in Education 2026The statutory safeguarding baseline from 1 September 2026; addresses AI within its four areas of online risk.Safeguarding, pupil use, filtering & monitoring, breaches
UK GDPR & Data Protection Act 2018 — as amended by the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025The core data protection law: lawful basis, roles, transfers, retention, automated decision-making, breach reporting.Data protection, impact assessment, acceptable use
ICO guidance on AI & the AI and data protection risk toolkitHow the regulator expects AI risk to data to be assessed and managed.Data protection, impact assessment
ICO Children’s CodeApplies wherever children’s data is processed: best interests first, high-privacy defaults, profiling off by default.Pupil use, data protection
Equality Act 2010 & the public sector equality dutyApplies to AI-assisted work as to everything else; bias and discrimination duties.Accuracy & oversight, acceptable use
DfE position on generative AI & the DfE generative AI product safety standards (January 2026)The Department’s expectations for safe AI products in education, including on training on personal data.Approved tools, impact assessment, product safety
JCQ guidance on AI use in assessmentsWhere qualifications are involved: malpractice, disclosure, and not relying on AI as the sole marker.Acceptable use, accuracy & oversight
DfE filtering & monitoring, cyber security, and digital leadership & governance standardsThe technical and governance standards that make tool approval enforceable and assured.Filtering & monitoring, roles & oversight
Online Safety Act 2023 & the Ofcom Protection of Children codeIllegal and harmful content duties, including AI-generated sexual imagery of children.Safeguarding & Prevent
Prevent duty guidance (2023) & Working Together to Safeguard Children 2026The counter-radicalisation duty and multi-agency safeguarding action.Safeguarding & Prevent, breaches
Academy Trust Handbook / scheme for financing schools / Schools Financial Value StandardThe financial governance framework that applies to procurement and oversight.Roles & oversight, approved tools

Frameworks and their commencement dates change. Confirm the current version of each one before you rely on it, and take your own professional or legal advice where there’s any doubt. This map is a signpost, not a statement of the law.
